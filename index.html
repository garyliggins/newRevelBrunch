About us

Who we are

The refreshing new vibe Hollywood has been waiting for. Incredible food, drinks, music and sports on a huge projector screen in an artistic, fly venue gives Revel Brunch & Day Lounge a one-of-a-kind atmosphere!

The Music Provides an effortless combination of the classic nostalgic energy of the 90's & 00's, Hip-Hop, R&B Afro-beats. The Drinks Featured on our unique menu are crafted by our expert mixologists at our Main bar, Bespoke bar or enjoy table-side.

Revel Brunch & Day Lounge has a 21+ age limit at all times.
Brunch hours are 12pm - 4pm
Day lounge hours are 4pm - 7pm

Bottle service reservations can be made for anytime between 12pm - 7pm and the table will be yours from your reservation time up until the end of the event at 7pm.

Brunch reservations are from 12pm - 4pm with a two hour time limit. If you wish to keep the table longer please inquire about adding bottle service to your reservation.
